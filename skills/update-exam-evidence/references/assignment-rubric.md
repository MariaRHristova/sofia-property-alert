# Assignment Rubric

The final submission is a publicly viewable Google Drive document, typically three to six pages, describing a small software project built with one or more of the AI development tools accepted by the course.

## Required content

1. Project idea and system requirements, up to one page.
2. System architecture divided into technological modules.
3. For every module, up to half a page covering:
   - Approach and reasoning
   - Step-by-step AI-assisted workflow
   - Testing strategy and validation
   - AI tool choice and reason
   - Two or three key prompts or interactions
4. Challenges and tool comparison:
   - Biggest challenges
   - Most helpful tool and why
   - Potential future improvements
5. At least two screenshots proving that the system works. UI, terminal, logs, and API-client output are acceptable.
6. A link to the source-code repository.

## Project-specific evidence targets

- Search-subscription UI and validation
- Persistence and data model
- Listing-provider or fixture-parser implementation
- Matching and deduplication
- Scheduled daily workflow
- Email delivery
- Automated tests and operational logs

The source assignment is `Project-Assignment.docx`. Preserve it unchanged.
